Northern Lights Air: Loyalty Program Analysis

📌 Project Overview:
This repository showcases an airline loyalty program project that analyzes the impact of a 2018 marketing campaign for Northern Lights Air, they're a Cabaduab airline, My goal was to determine if the Feburary-April promotion was successful in driving up the loyalty program enrollment and increased flight volume during the summer

📊 Key Insights & Business Strategy
-The Bachelor: While flight frequency was consistent across all demographics 30 flights/year, the Bachelor-educated segment represents the core of the business, accounting for over 317,000 total flights.

-The "Star" Power: Star Loyalty Card holders are the highest volume drivers.

-The Upsell Opportunity: Because flight frequency is capped across all tiers, the primary growth lever is tier migration (upselling Star members to Aurora/Nova) to increase fee-based revenue.

-Campaign Success: The April promotion directly correlated with a 44% increase in summer flight volume compared to the previous year.

🛠️ What I did and the tools i used:

Extraction: Utilized SQL and Google BigQuery to pull and audit 16,737 customer records and 100k+ flight activity rows.

Cleaning & Analysis: Managed the "hellscape" of raw data using Python (Pandas) to perform data merging, auditing, and trend analysis.

Visualization: Developed an executive-ready Tableau Dashboard to translate technical findings into actionable business visuals.
